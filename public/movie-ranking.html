My top 3 movies of all time

The lunch box

This is an Indian movie starring Irrfan khan and Nimrat Kaur.
An unlikely mistake by a tiffin carrier service results in Ila's tiffin, that was made for her husband, being delivered to Saajan Fernandes. An unusual friendship soon develops between the two.

Jab We Met

An Indian movie starring Shahid kapoor and Kareena kapoor.
Aditya, a heartbroken business tycoon, aimlessly boards a train to escape his depressing life. He meets Geet, a bubbly Punjabi girl ,and gets pulled into her crazy life.

The vow

Paige meets with a terrible accident which leaves her in a coma. When she wakes up, she doesn't recognise her husband Leo, who then tries to win her back by courting her again.
